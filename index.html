    About Jazzyspoon

    Hello Friends!


    Jazzyspoon (aka Jason Pottorff) is an electronic musician/producer/sound designer/remixer currently based out of Denver, Colorado USA. Preferring not to be confined to one subgenre or musical style makes it difficult to pinpoint a category for his music, you’ll likely find his biggest fanbase is comprised mostly of hardcore audiophiles and other electronic musicians.


    He is best known for continually experimenting in his studio to find the perfect hybrid of IDM, downtempo, glitch, trip hop, rock, shoegaze, industrial, ambient, electro, dubstep, breaks, jazz, club, and so on. Enjoying the artistic exploration process so much more rewarding than commercial success, he might almost hope to never find that perfect hybrid so that he may continue creatively growing and learning new techniques.

    Generally releasing only one Jazzyspoon CD every year or two for both personal and quality purposes, he can regularly be found sharing his current musical explorations and knowledge on forums across the net.


    "I will keep posting more important tracks, videos, and information on my Website for all of you. Please give your support and love on social media." - Jazzyspoon
